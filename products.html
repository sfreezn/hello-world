Unruh Manufacturing provides custom welding services for steel, stainless steel and aluminum. We have TIG capability for those higher quality, more precision welding jobs. We also sandblast and paint for high quality finishes.

Some of our custom fabrication jobs include:

  • Field dollies
  • Fertilizer belt rebuilds
  • Truck transfer tub rebuilds
  • Tractor tank saddles
  • Rice roller fabrication
  • Tractor belly pans
  • Swather trailer
  • Aluminum control pedestal for fishing boat

Our berm buster scatters harvest trash after the pickup machine comes through. The distance the trash is scattered is adjustable.
